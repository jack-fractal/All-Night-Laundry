"Hey," says Emmie. "I'm not sure I'm totally following what you two are up too, but this does not mean Agent Miller is going to die. The people working on her know what they're doing, and we got to her fast. Like - two minutes fast. She had the good sense to get shot inside a hospital."

"I know."

"Being shot [i]is[/i] bad, but people survive it every day."

"I [i]know[/i]," says Bina. "I just wanted - I guess - not to have to worry about her."

"You know," says Emmie. "I heard somewhere that worrying about something that you have no control over seldom helps."

"Oh yeah?" says Bina.

"It's what I heard."

"Well tell that to my anxiety disorder."

"Uhhh, Hey!" says Amie, slowly. "I can't remember which pizza place the pizza came from."

"I don't think so. How is she supposed to send the right pizza if she doesn't know where to get it from?"

"She'll figure it out," says Bina. "Probably best she [i]doesn't[/i] know. The less predestination she's working off, the less paradox this little time machine will have to tank."
