Time stretches and blurs. How long has she been running? It's only five or six meters away. It feels like she's been running for days.

At some point, she runs past [i]herself[/i] running in the opposite direction. 

[img]0899_a.png[/img]

"I don't remember that," she thinks, which is true, but only for an instant. The next instant, things change, as icy splinters of memory embed themselves in her mind.

This place is getting less survivable.
